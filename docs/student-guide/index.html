Comprehensive Rules & Student Guide

Welcome to Science Around the Board. This is a competitive strategy game where your knowledge of the course material directly translates to in-game success.

1. The Objectiveâ€‹

There are two ways to win. Your instructor will tell you which to prioritize:

  1. Mastery Victory: Capture all 4 Corner Milestones by passing their expert exams. This proves total subject mastery.
  2. Financial Victory: Have the highest Net Worth (Cash + Assets) when the game timer ends.

2. Setup Instructionsâ€‹

This game runs in your browser. No installation is required.

  1. Download Materials: Get the questions.tsv file (and optional images) from your instructor.
  2. Launch: Go to the game URL.
  3. Upload:
    • Click "Upload Custom Questions" and select the .tsv file.
    • (If Encrypted): Enter the class password provided by your instructor.
    • (If Images used): Click "Upload Images" and select ALL image files at once.
  4. Team Setup: Select your player count (1-4 teams).
  5. Topic Choice: Select the topic to be played
  6. Complete Pre-Survey
  7. Start the Game!

3. Gameplay Mechanicsâ€‹

Movement & Propertiesâ€‹

  • Roll Dice: Move your token.
  • Unowned Property: If you land on a research topic nobody owns, you can attempt to "Publish" (buy) it.
    • The Quiz: You must answer a question correctly.
    • Success: You can choose to pay the cost and own the tile.
    • Failure: You pay a small penalty and do not get the tile.
  • Owned Property (Rival): If you land on a tile owned by another team, you must pay Rent.

Rent Defense (The "50% Rule")â€‹

When you owe rent, you have a chance to defend your funding:

  • The Defense Question: You must answer a question related to that topic.
  • Correct: You proved your knowledge! You pay only 50% of the rent.
  • Incorrect: You pay the Full (100%) rent.

ðŸ§ª Upgrades (The "Monopoly" Mechanic)â€‹

You can upgrade your labs to charge massive rent, but strict rules apply:

  1. Group Ownership: You must own ALL tiles of a specific color group (e.g., all 3 "Side 1" tiles).
  2. Even Building: You must upgrade evenly. You cannot build a Level 3 tile if the others in the group are still Level 0.
  3. How to Build: Click the "Lab Manager" button to buy upgrades and multiply the rent charged to other players.

âš¡ Chaos Tokensâ€‹

Chaos Tokens introduce "Take That" mechanics to the game.

  • How to get them: Earn one by capturing a Milestone. After all Milestone tiles are owned, chaos tokens can be purchased for $500.
  • How to use them: Click "Use Chaos" -> Select a rival's property.
  • The Duel: You face a question related to the tile you are trying to steal:
    • Success: You STEAL the property for 50% of its value.
    • Failure: You pay a fine to the bank.

4. Crisis Managementâ€‹

Bankruptcyâ€‹

If you run out of money (Negative Balance):

  1. Liquidation: The game will force you to sell assets or downgrade labs until you are positive.
  2. The Bailout (Grant): If you sell everything and are still in debt, you apply for an Emergency Grant.
    • The Cost: You receive $500 to keep playing.
    • The Penalty: You are disqualified from the Mastery Victory. You can still play, but you cannot win the main objective.

5. Submission (Mandatory)â€‹

This game generates a Grade Report.

  1. To end the game, click on "End Game" in the top-right.
    • IMPORTANT: Do NOT click on "Exit Session" unless you want to exit your current game without saved files.
  2. Complete the Post-Survey, which include Confidence sliders and 10 questions.
  3. On the Summary Screen, click "Export CSV".
  4. Save the file as TeamName_Session.csv.
  5. Upload this file to your course page.
Do Not Close the Tab!

Your game data lives in the browser's temporary memory. If you close the tab or refresh the page before downloading the CSV, your data is lost forever and cannot be recovered.
